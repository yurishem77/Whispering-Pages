Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a commitment period?

No, all memberships are month-to-month or year-to-year with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime.

How does the borrowing system work?

You can borrow physical books from our library and have them shipped to your door. Return them using our prepaid shipping labels within 30 days.

Do membership discounts apply to all purchases?

Yes, your membership discount applies to all book purchases, including new releases and special editions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and UPI payments. All payments are processed securely.
