Book Genres

Discover books across a wide range of genres, carefully curated for every reader. Click on any genre to explore.

Fantasy

Stories set in imaginary universes, often featuring magic, mythical creatures and epic quests.

Science Fiction

Speculative stories that explore futuristic concepts, advanced technology, space exploration or alternative realities.

Mystery/Crime

Narrative centered around the investigation and solution of a crime or a puzzle, typically led by a detective or sleuth.

Thriller/Suspense

Plot-driven stories, designed to evoke excitement and anticipation, often involving dangerous situations, espionage or psychological tension.

Horror

Fiction intended to frighten or shock the reader, focusing on themes of death, evil or the supernatural.

Romance

Stories where the central plot focuses on the development of a romantic relationship between the main characters, typically with an emotionally satisfying resolution.

Historical Fiction

Fictional narratives set in a specific time in the past, often weaving fictional characters and events around real historical figures or periods.

Young Adult

Readers aged around 12-18 with teenage protagonists, focusing on relatable coming-of-age themes like self-discovery, first love and spanning genres like fantasy, sci-fi, romance, contemporary and dystopian fiction.

Kids Section

Focusing on learning, imagination and emotional growth through engaging stories, characters and formats.

Religion

Diverse category encompassing non-fiction works that explore belief systems, moral frameworks, spiritual practices and cultural customs.

Narrative Non-Fiction

Tells true stories using literary styles and techniques, making factual information engaging and immersive like a novel, with elements like plot, character development and dialogue, but remaining meticulously accurate.

Self Help

Non-fiction guides aimed at personal improvement, offering practical advice and actionable steps for readers to solve problems and enhance various aspects of their lives.

Cooking

Encompasses books focused on food preparation, techniques and culture, including instructional cookbooks, culinary memoirs, regional cuisine guides and recipes.
